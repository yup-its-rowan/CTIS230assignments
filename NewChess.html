GOSPEL OF THREE QUARTERS CHESS

  • Only three quarters of the board are usable
  • No piece may travel over no-mans land
  • Pawns may move and attack in the ways turning the corner in the corner quarter but no other
  • Pawns may not move back into previous corners
  • Kings may capture pieces by jumping over them
  • Knights and pawns may occupy the same square, allowing the pawn to ride along
  • Knight Riders cannot attack while grouped up
  • Bishop interactions will be determined by the player who screams the loudest
  • If a knight or a knight rider lands beside a knight and a d10 gives a 6 or higher, the other knight is converted to your army
  • Pieces may be captured if they are surrounded by a number of pawns, which will all conglomerate onto one square
  • A bipawn will act like a pawn with twice the movement and attack length, with tripawn and quadpawn in similar
  • Bipawns, tripawns, and quadpawns may split up, as may knight riders
